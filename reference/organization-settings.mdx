## Delete organization

<Warning>
This is a destructive action. After you delete your organization, you lose access to all data within that org.
</Warning>

### Prerequisites

Before you delete your organization, ensure the following:

- You have backed up any data you want to keep. You can't access the data after deletion.
- If you're on a paid plan, downgrade to the Personal plan first. For more information, see [Downgrade plan](#downgrade-plan).
- If you're on a trial, the trial has ended or you have downgraded to a free plan.

### Delete organization

To delete your organization:

1. Click <img src="/doc-assets/icons/settings.svg" className="inline-icon" alt="Settings icon" /> **Settings > General**.
1. Click **Delete organization**.
1. Follow the prompts to confirm deletion.

<Note>
If the **Delete organization** button is unavailable, you may need to downgrade your plan first. Go to <img src="/doc-assets/icons/settings.svg" className="inline-icon" alt="Settings icon" /> **Settings > Plan** and downgrade to the Personal plan. For more information, see [Upgrade or downgrade plan](/reference/usage-billing#upgrade-or-downgrade-plan).
</Note>
