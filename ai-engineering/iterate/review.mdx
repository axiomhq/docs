Use Review to inspect production conversations, record expert feedback, and route recurring failures into issues.

## Open review

To open review:

1. Open **AI engineering**.
1. Click **Review**.
1. Select a capability from the sidebar.

## Choose a queue

Use the queue selector in the page header to change the review source.

- **Flagged** shows traces or conversations that a teammate marked for review.
- **Recent** shows the latest conversations for the selected capability.
- **Negative feedback** shows traces with negative user feedback.
- **Errored** shows traces with errors or exceptions.
- **Reviewed** shows conversations that already have a saved review.

## Flag a trace for review

Conversations can be flagged for review through the trace viewer. To flag a trace for review:

1. Open the trace in Axiom.
1. In the trace sidebar, click **Flag for review**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/doc-assets/shots/ai-engineering/flag-for-review.png" alt="Flag trace for review" />
</Frame>


To open the flagged queue:

1. Open **AI engineering** > **Review**.
1. Select the **Flagged** queue.

## Review a conversation

To review a conversation:

1. Select a conversation from the queue list.
1. Inspect the conversation in the center panel.
1. Optional: Open the **Detail** tab to inspect conversation metadata.
1. In the **Review** panel, set **Quality** to **Good** or **Bad**.
1. Enter your notes and submit.


After submission, Axiom processes the review and does one of the following:

- Matches the review to an existing issue.
- Creates a new unresolved issue.
- Skips issue creation for positive, unrelated, or vague feedback.

If Axiom finds or creates issues, the confirmation panel links to the affected issue records.

If you don't agree with the automatic decision you can click **Edit** and select a different
issue or create a new one.

## Move through the queue

To continue working through a queue:

1. Click **Done & Next** to open the next queued conversation.
1. Click **Next** to skip the current item without saving a review.

## Reopen completed reviews

To inspect saved reviews:

1. Open **AI engineering** > **Review**.
1. Select the same capability.
1. Choose the **Reviewed** queue.
1. Select a conversation from the list.

The review panel is read-only in the reviewed queue.

## Keyboard shortcuts

Use these shortcuts while reviewing:

- Press `G` to mark the conversation as good.
- Press `B` to mark the conversation as bad.
- Press `J` to move to the next item.
- Press `Cmd` + `Enter` on macOS, or `Ctrl` + `Enter` on other systems, to submit the review.
- Press `Esc` to close the result panel.
