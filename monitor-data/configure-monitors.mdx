## Edit monitors

To edit an existing monitor:

1. Click the Monitors tab.
1. Click the monitor in the list that you want to edit.
1. In the top right, click **Edit monitor**.
1. Make changes to the monitor.
1. Click **Save**.

## Disable monitors

Disable a monitor to prevent it from running for a specific amount of time.

To disable a monitor:

1. Click the Monitors tab.
1. Click the monitor in the list that you want to disable.
1. In the top right, click **Disable monitor**.
1. Select the time period for which you want to disable the monitor.
1. Click **Disable monitor**.

Axiom automatically enables the monitor after the time period you specified.

## Enable monitors

To enable a monitor:

1. Click the Monitors tab.
1. Click the monitor in the list that you want to enable.
1. In the top right, click **Enable monitor**.
1. Click **Enable monitor**.

## Clone monitors

To clone a monitor:

1. Click the Monitors tab.
1. Click the monitor in the list that you want to delete.
1. In the top right, click <img src="/doc-assets/icons/ellipsis-vertical.svg" className="inline-icon" alt="More" /> **More**.
1. Click **Clone monitor**.

## Delete monitors

To delete a monitor:

1. Click the Monitors tab.
1. Click the monitor in the list that you want to delete.
1. In the top right, click <img src="/doc-assets/icons/ellipsis-vertical.svg" className="inline-icon" alt="More" /> **More**.
1. Click **Delete monitor**.
