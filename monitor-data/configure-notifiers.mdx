## Disable notifiers

Disable a monitor to prevent it from running for a specific amount of time.

To disable a notifier:
1. Click the Monitors tab.
1. In the left, click **Notifiers**.
1. Click the notifier in the list that you want to disable.
1. In the top right, click **Disable notifier**.
1. Select the time period for which you want to disable the notifier.
1. Click **Disable**.

Axiom automatically enables the notifier after the time period you specified.

## Enable notifiers

To enable a notifier:
1. Click the Monitors tab.
1. In the left, click **Notifiers**.
1. Click the notifier in the list that you want to enable.
1. In the top right, click **Enable notifier**.
1. Click **Enable**.

## Delete notifiers

To delete a notifier:
1. Click the Monitors tab.
1. In the left, click **Notifiers**.
1. Click the notifier in the list that you want to delete.
1. In the top right, click <img src="/doc-assets/icons/delete.svg" className="inline-icon" alt="Delete icon" />.
1. Click **Delete**.
