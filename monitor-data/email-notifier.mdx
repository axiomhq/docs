To create an email notifier, follow these steps:

1. Click the **Monitors** tab, and then click **Manage notifiers** on the right.
1. Click **New notifier** on the top right.
1. Name your notifier.
1. Click **Email**.
1. In the **Users** section, add the email addresses where you want to send notifications, and then click **+** on the right.
1. Click **Create**.
